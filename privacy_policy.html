Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Warring States Card (the "App") handles your information when you use the App. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

The App is designed to minimize data collection:

  • No Personal Information. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any personally identifiable information.
  • Guest Login Only. The App uses an anonymous guest login — a random player ID is generated locally. No account registration is required.
  • No Analytics SDK. We do not use Firebase, Google Analytics, or any third-party analytics framework. We do not track your activity across other apps or websites.

2. Automatically Collected Data

The following minimal data is collected solely for game functionality:

  • Game Data. Your game results (wins/losses), rank/tier, and match history are stored on our backend server to enable matchmaking and leaderboards.
  • Purchase Receipts. If you make an in-app purchase, the purchase token/receipt is sent to our backend for verification. We never process or store payment card information — all payments are handled by Apple's App Store or Google Play.

3. Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Device identifiers (such as Android Advertising ID or iOS IDFA)
  • IP address
  • General location (city-level, not precise GPS)

AdMob uses this data to serve personalized or contextual ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

If you purchase the Remove Ads in-app product, all advertisements will be permanently removed from the App, and AdMob will no longer collect data from your use of the App.

4. In-App Purchases

The App offers the following consumable and non-consumable purchases:

  • Gold Biscuit packs (virtual currency for in-game card packs)
  • Remove Ads (permanently removes advertisements)
  • Battle Pass Premium (unlocks premium reward track)

All transactions are processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We do not have access to your payment card details. Purchase receipts are sent to our backend solely for fulfillment and fraud prevention.

5. Data Storage & Retention

  • Local Data. Your game progress is saved locally on your device via Hive database. You can delete this data at any time by uninstalling the App.
  • Server Data. Anonymous game records (player ID, rank, match history) are stored on our backend server hosted on Railway. You may request deletion of server-side data by contacting us (see section 8).

6. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob — advertising (see section 3)
  • Apple App Store / Google Play — in-app purchase processing
  • Railway — backend server hosting

7. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.
